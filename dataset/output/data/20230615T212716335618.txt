Located in the iconic Deansgate area at the southern end of Manchester city centre, all four towers enjoy unrivalled views across the city and countryside. This deluxe duplex penthouse occupies the top floors of East Tower, boasting exceptional dual aspect views of the city and Castlefield. With over 3,125 sq ft of carefully designed living space, every last detail has been considered. The penthouse comprises three spacious double bedrooms, each with en-suite. The master bedroom features bespoke dressing areas and a luxurious free-standing steel bath, while the second bedroom has premium fitted wardrobes with sliding doors. The expansive open-plan living and dining area is the perfect space for entertaining guests or relaxing. The SieMatic kitchen benefits from integrated Siemens appliances, plus a warming drawer and Quooker tap. There is Havwoods Bronzo narrow plank engineered oak flooring in a herringbone pattern to the living area and hallways, with carpet to all bedrooms, and fully-tiled en-suites and WC. There is air-conditioning and electric dim-out roller blinds throughout. As Manchester's premier landmark development, Deansgate Square has over 92,000 sq ft of private amenity space, unlike any other residential development in the UK. At 50,000 sq ft, The Club offers exceptional health and wellness facilities. Residents can enjoy a 20m pool, spin and yoga studios, state-of-the-art gym, as well as fully-sized sports hall, lounge and an extensive rooftop garden. The Concierge and Business gallery also offers a spectrum of spaces to cater for all needs including co-working and lounge areas, a boardroom, private pods and x3 multi-use suites, which are equally perfect for entertaining, dining or gaming with friends in the evenings and weekends.
