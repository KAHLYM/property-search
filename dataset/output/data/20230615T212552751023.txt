Description This unique house was developed in 2008 as a private commission for the current owner. It comprises approximately 11,010 sq ft (inclusive of garage and plant areas), and has an exceptional south facing garden plot width of approximately 92' / 28.11m . The property offers wonderful lateral living, with a lift to all floors, including a large reception room that opens onto the garden, a gym, swimming pool and cinema room, plus a double garage and off street parking. Location Pitt Street is a quiet road situated on Campden Hill, north of Kensington High Street, between Holland Park to the West, and Kensington Gardens to the East. The High Street, which is a few minutes' walk away, offers a good range of useful shopping including Waitrose, Marks & Spencer and Whole Foods. In addition, Notting Hill Gate is situated a little further away to the north with an additional selection of shops. Kensington is regarded as an excellent family area, with better gardens and open spaces,an excellent range of schools including Fox's and St Mary Abbot's Primary schoolds, Holland Park Academy, Pembridge Hall, Thomas's and Wetherby Prp schools. Points of interest are the new Design Museum at the southern end of Holland Park, and the renowned Portobello market in Notting Hill. The wider area offers an excellent range of restaurants, and good communications via bus routes and underground stations at both Kensington High Street and Notting Hill Gate (Circle and District & Central Lines).
