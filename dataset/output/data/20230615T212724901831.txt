This unique detached home sits on stilts and offers panoramic views across the beautiful gardens, private pond, woodland and the Mersey. Accessed via a private road you are greeted with secure electric gating. There is a large driveway providing ample off road parking and an integral garage. Internally the property comprises, entrance hallway, open plan living room with balcony and uninterrupted views, stylish fitted kitchen with island, integrated appliances and dining area, with separate utility room. An inner hallway leads to a contemporary family bathroom, two double bedrooms, one with walk in dressing room and ensuite. To the first floor are two further double bedrooms, a four piece bathroom and an additional room currently utilised as a comfortable reception room, but would also make a fantastic bedroom. The property offers stunning gardens and outside space. There is a covered terrace area ideal for alfresco dining, large lawn, landscaped planted borders, private pond and surrounded by mature woodland. Situated in a highly desirable Didsbury location the property is ideally positioned to take advantage of some excellent local amenties, schools and walks. Didsbury village and the trendy car bars and restaurants of West Didsbury are all close by. There are some excellent transport links, including easy access to the M60 motorway network, major bus routes and tram and train.
