The accommodation on offer is over three floors and comprises of a welcoming reception hallway. Fantastic family sized lounge, which gives access via double French doors to the private garden. There is an excellent size kitchen with integrated oven, hob and hood and range of base and wall mounted storage units providing a lovely setting for family dining. Also situated on this level is a shower room. The first floor offers three good sized bedrooms with storage and a family bathroom with shower over bath. Situated on the top floor is the master bedroom which is enhanced with a spacious balcony and stunning views over the River Clyde. Also on this level is the family bathroom with a three-piece suite and shower over bath. The property has been environmentally designed with a communal heating system, solar panels and double glazing all contributing to lower the home’s running costs. The property benefits from private front and rear gardens and a driveway. Ample visitor parking is available nearby. London Avenue is well situated for local shopping which can be found in the Forge Shopping Centre as well as the Clyde Walkway and Cuningar Loop Woodland Park. There are also various sporting facilities nearby such as the Emirates Arena, Sir Chris Hoy Velodrome, Tollcross Swimming Pool. There is schooling both at primary and secondary levels in the area. Motorway links and public transport including bus and rail provide easy access to the city centre and surrounding areas which has a host of bars, cinemas, restaurants, theatres, universities and hospitals.
