Key features 1.00 acres Detached Parking Property description The site offers the potential to create an enclave of bespoke homes in a popular residential area. Approached down a quiet single track road, the site is situated in a pleasant leafy residential location with an attractive outlook. Comprising a site of approximately 1 acre which is part level, holding one single detached house and summer house, part with a sloping gradient falling away towards the southerly boundary. There is an overall fall across the site of around 10-12 metres, and as such will require some degree of cut and fill work prior to construction work beginning. The site has lapsed planning for 7 dwellings granted in 2007 (Planning Reference 07/55661/FUL). This includes three 5 bedroom linked detached houses of 200m2 and four 4 bedroom detached houses of 160m2, though it may be that the eventual purchaser looks to pursue a different proposal. The property is held freehold under Title Number GM34621. We are informed VAT is not applicable. Photography is for illustrative purposes only. Viewings by appointment with the sole agent, Knight Frank. The site is situated at the end of Kersal Bank which runs off Radford Street within close proximity to Bury New Road (A56) and within close proximity to Sedgley Park, Salford and Manchester. Prestwich Village including Prestwich Metrolink Station is approx. 1 mile to the north. Prestwich has become an extremely popular and trendy place to live with commuters and includes a variety of shops, bars and restaurants. Manchester City Centre is approx. 2 miles to the south.
