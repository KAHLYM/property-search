Situated on a substantial plot and rarely available on one of DIDSBURYS SOUGHT AFTER ROADS, Rowsley Avenue is within easy walking distance to trendy Burton Road and the open green space of River Mersey. The accommodation expands to 2461 sq ft, covering three floors and boasts five well-proportioned bedrooms. Externally there is a SECURELY GATED DRIVEWAY with ample parking for several vehicles and a PRIVATE LANDSCAPED SOUTH EASTERLY FACING GARDEN. This stunning property is entered through a beautiful wooden front door, leading into the entrance hallway which showcases the grand broad, turning staircase. Off the hallway, to the left is a bay fronted reception room boasting a beautifully detailed corniced ceiling, whilst to the right is a bright and spacious living room offering dual aspect windows which floods the room with natural light and a feature wood-burning stove. To the rear of the property is an open plan living/dining kitchen which provides a feature centre island with space for bar stools, sleek handle-less wall and base level units with a splendid Aga cooker. The kitchen opens up into a further living/dining space which hosts a further wood-burning stove with exposed brick surround. Double doors lead out onto the rear garden. Additionally to this floor there is W.C and utility room. To the first floor, a generous landing spans off into three spacious bedrooms, all bedrooms offer ample space for free standing or fitted bedroom furniture. To this floor there is a fully tiled art-deco four piece, family bathroom suite. The top floor offers two further well-proportioned bedrooms, both providing ample space for furniture and served by a three piece bathroom suite with Velux window. Externally the property is approached via electronically controlled gates which opens up into the gravel paved driveway providing ample parking for several vehicles. To the rear is an impressive South Easterly facing enclosed garden. Offering a high level of privacy, which is mainly laid with lawn with an area of stone paving, perfectly suited for outdoor table and chairs which will have you dreaming of relaxing outdoors.
